## Hero

### Headline

Deliverability Operating System for Outbound Revenue Teams

### Subheadline

Sovereign Engine gives founders, RevOps teams, and outbound agencies a control plane for domain protection, inbox placement visibility, reputation scoring, queue telemetry, and worker scaling.

### Founder / CTO Narrative

Outbound teams often treat deliverability as a collection of scripts, inboxes, warmup rules, and fragile sender limits. That works until a domain burns, inbox placement drops, campaigns stall, and pipeline slows down.

Sovereign Engine reframes deliverability as revenue infrastructure. It tracks domain health, queue pressure, worker activity, provider lanes, and reputation events so teams can slow down risky sends before they damage domain trust.

The asset is positioned for a buyer who wants to own a deliverability control plane instead of spending months building queue orchestration, reputation monitoring, dashboards, worker telemetry, and acquisition-ready documentation from scratch.
