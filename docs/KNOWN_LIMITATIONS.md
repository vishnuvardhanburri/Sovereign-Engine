# Sovereign Engine Known Limits

Sovereign Engine is demo-ready with mock-safe proof data and production-ready after the buyer connects the external assets that must belong to them.

## Buyer Inputs Required

- Buyer-owned sending domains and DNS access.
- Verified SMTP or ESP credentials.
- Email validation provider key.
- Lawful contact data, suppression list, unsubscribe policy, and physical mailing address where required.
- Public dashboard domain with HTTPS and production secrets.

## Safe Claims

- Provider-aware pacing and lane-level reputation controls.
- Mock-safe stress testing without sending real email.
- Tamper-evident privileged-action audit logs.
- Health proof for Redis, Postgres, BullMQ, and worker heartbeats.
- Production gate that locks real sending until buyer inputs are connected.

## Not Claimed

- No guarantee of inbox placement.
- No bypassing provider policies.
- No sending without lawful data and opt-out handling.
- No automatic creation of buyer-owned DNS, SMTP, or legal/business assets.

## Recommended Handoff Language

Use this phrasing in listings and calls:

```text
Sovereign Engine is a compliance-first deliverability operating system. It is demo-ready today and production-ready once the buyer connects verified domains, SMTP/ESP credentials, validation keys, and compliant contact data.
```
